## 2. Who We Are / About Us

Thanks to our extensive 3D printing services, laser sintering technologies, and software systems, we have provided and continue to provide rapid prototyping services to countless companies across various industrial sectors such as aerospace, defense, medical, automotive, manufacturing, and mold-making. Our long-standing and growing client base, built over more than 14 years of working with 3D modeling technology, is a testament to the trust we have earned.

## 3. Key Services

- **Additive Manufacturing:** Also known as 3D printing, additive manufacturing offers numerous significant advantages that are revolutionizing the world of production by reducing prototyping costs in both single-part and small-batch production.
- **Selective Laser Sintering (SLS):** Create functional parts directly from 3D modeling data. Commonly used for durable polymer prototypes.
- **Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS):** Supports metal prototypes where strength and temperature performance are required.
- **Industrial 3D Printing for Molding:** Integrated functionality and faster additive manufacturing enhance both product quality and productivity in molding tools.
- **3D Modeling:** Professional 3D modeling and design services tailored for industrial rapid prototyping and manufacturing.
